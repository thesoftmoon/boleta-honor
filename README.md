# Boletaz

**A simple Chilean Honor Ticket calculator**

Is a React app to calculate the values to input in your Chilean Invoice fees (in chile is known by Boleta de Honorarios or honors ticket), it calculate the gross and liquid values, the gross values are the fees in total (legal taxes plus your desired amount) the legal taxes are currently in Chile 13% for this type of invoice. The liquid value are your desired value, directly to your pocket plus the legal taxes, the gross value is slightly more than the liquid value after tax added.
